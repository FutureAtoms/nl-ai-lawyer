# Dutch Contract Review

Perform a comprehensive clause-by-clause review of a Dutch contract, applying risk-based traffic-light flags.

## Skill and Tools

Use skill: **dutch-contract-review**

Use MCP tools where applicable:
- `legislation_search` to look up relevant BW (Burgerlijk Wetboek) provisions
- `caselaw_search_rechtspraak` to find relevant jurisprudence for flagged clauses

## Workflow

### Step 1: Contract Identification

- Determine the contract type (koopovereenkomst, huurovereenkomst, arbeidsovereenkomst, dienstverleningsovereenkomst, licentieovereenkomst, etc.)
- Identify the parties and their roles
- Determine applicable BW provisions (Boek 6 general obligations, Boek 7 specific named contracts)
- If the `position` argument is provided, frame the analysis from that party's perspective

### Step 2: Clause-by-Clause Extraction and Analysis

For each clause in the contract:

1. **Extract** the clause text
2. **Classify** the clause category (definitions, scope, price/payment, delivery, liability, indemnification, IP, confidentiality, term/termination, dispute resolution, force majeure, etc.)
3. **Assess risk** and assign a flag:
   - **GREEN** -- Standard/favorable: clause is market-standard, balanced, or favorable to the user's position
   - **YELLOW** -- Needs attention: clause deviates from standard practice, contains ambiguity, or could be improved
   - **RED** -- High risk/non-standard: clause is one-sided, potentially unenforceable, or exposes significant risk
4. **Explain** why the flag was assigned, referencing applicable BW articles

### Step 3: BW Compliance Check

- Check compliance with mandatory provisions in BW Boek 6 (general contract law) and Boek 7 (specific contract types)
- Verify whether any clauses attempt to derogate from dwingend recht (mandatory law)
- For consumer contracts: check against afdeling 6.5.3 (algemene voorwaarden) and the grey/black lists (art. 6:236 and 6:237 BW)
- For B2B contracts: check reasonableness under art. 6:233 BW

### Step 4: Algemene Voorwaarden Compliance

- Determine if general terms and conditions are incorporated
- Check if they were properly provided (ter hand gesteld) per art. 6:234 BW
- Review for clauses on the black list (art. 6:236 BW) -- always void
- Review for clauses on the grey list (art. 6:237 BW) -- presumed unreasonable
- Flag any battle-of-the-forms issues (art. 6:225 lid 3 BW)

### Step 5: Missing Clause Analysis

Identify essential clauses that are absent but should be present for this contract type, such as:
- Force majeure (overmacht) provisions
- Limitation of liability (beperking aansprakelijkheid)
- Intellectual property assignment/license terms
- Data protection / AVG compliance clause (verwerkersovereenkomst if applicable)
- Dispute resolution (choice of forum, arbitration, mediation)
- Governing law clause
- Confidentiality provisions
- Anti-assignment clause
- Entire agreement (gehele overeenkomst) clause
