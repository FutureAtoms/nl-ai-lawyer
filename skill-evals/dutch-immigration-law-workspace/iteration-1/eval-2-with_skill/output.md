## Requirements

**The income must be:**

- **Sufficient** (voldoende): at least 100% of the net minimum wage
- **Durable** (duurzaam): your employment contract must be for at least 1 year from the date of application, or you must demonstrate 3 years of sufficient income history

**What does NOT count:**

- Study finance (studiefinanciering)

**Note for Dutch citizen sponsors:** The income requirement applies to all sponsors, including Dutch citizens. However, if you receive social assistance (bijstand), you will not meet the income requirement. There is no exemption for Dutch citizens from the income requirement for family reunification (unlike in some other EU Member States).
